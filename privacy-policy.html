Privacy Policy

for the Bitpie Clipboard Chrome extension
Effective: September 4, 2025

Summary

We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data. Bitpie Clipboard generates secure passwords, creative usernames, and hashtags directly in your browser. The generated results stay on your device unless you copy them yourself.

The Chrome Web Store may require a privacy policy when an extension declares certain permissions or interacts with remote services. This document explains what our extension does and why those permissions exist.

Data We Collect

None. We do not collect, track, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Browsing history, keystrokes, or page content
  • Analytics, crash reports, advertising identifiers

All generation (passwords, usernames, hashtags) happens locally in your browser. If you press “Copy”, the content is placed onto your system clipboard via the standard Clipboard API and is not sent to us.

Permissions & Why We Need Them

PermissionWhy it’s neededWhat it does not do
storage Saves small local preferences such as last-used options. This improves convenience across sessions. Does not send data off your device. No personal data is stored or shared.
tabs Allows the extension to open a new tab only when a remote setting explicitly tells it to (e.g., show a related help page). Does not read your browsing history, tab contents, or track which sites you visit.
host_permissions
https://firestore.googleapis.com/*		 Lets the extension fetch a tiny, read-only configuration value from our Firebase project. That value decides whether to show the built-in tools or load a specific page from our site. Cannot read website content or user data. We do not upload any user information to Firebase.

We do not execute arbitrary remote code. The remote fetch is a simple JSON document (feature flag/URL). No eval, no third-party scripts injected into pages.

Third-Party Content

In some cases, the extension may open or embed a page from our own website (deskappsdev.github.io/BitpieClipBoard). That website may have its own cookies or logs—see its policy if applicable. This occurs only when the Firebase configuration instructs the extension to show that page.

Security

We follow least-privilege principles and avoid collecting data entirely. Generated content remains local. No analytics SDKs are included.

Children’s Privacy

Bitpie Clipboard does not target children and does not collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the effective date above and publish the new version at this URL.
